About us

Welcome to Boy, where tradition meets contemporary style. We are a brand dedicated to crafting exquisite shirts and pants by hand, ensuring each piece is unique and of the highest quality. Our journey began with a passion for fine craftsmanship and a desire to create clothing that stands out.

At Boy, we believe in the beauty of handmade garments. Each shirt and pair of pants is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans, using the finest materials. This dedication to quality and detail ensures that our clothing is not just fashion, but a statement of individuality and elegance.

Our mission is to provide you with clothing that not only looks good but feels good too. We are committed to sustainable practices and ethical production, ensuring that every piece is made with respect for both people and the planet. Join us in celebrating the art of handcrafting and experience the difference that true craftsmanship makes.
